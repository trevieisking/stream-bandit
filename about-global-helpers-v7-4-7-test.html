What Stream Bandit does

For viewers

Browse movies, open clean Details, play in Player 1, continue watching, and save Watchlist/Favourite/Like picks.

For accessibility

Player comfort, readable layout, stronger focus visibility, louder audio controls and global readability tools stay protected.

For creators

Channels, playlists, collections, submissions and review tools are part of the wider platform plan.

For safety

Readiness checks, policy routes, admin-only tools and staged checkpoints keep risky actions away from public pages.
