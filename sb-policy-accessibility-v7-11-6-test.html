STREAM BANDIT ACCESSIBILITY STATEMENT — DRAFT Stream Bandit aims to provide readable, comfortable and accessible video controls. Current priorities include clear text, strong contrast, simple navigation, keyboard-friendly controls, player comfort settings, audio boost, captions or subtitles where available, and easy access to accessibility settings. Stream Bandit should preserve louder audio and player comfort features because they are important to users with hearing needs. Users can report accessibility problems or request improvements by emailing info@chatterfriendsstreambandit.co.uk. This statement is a working draft and should be reviewed before public launch.
