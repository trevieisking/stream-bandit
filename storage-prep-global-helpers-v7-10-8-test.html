Overview

Purpose

Keep storage decisions clear before future uploader, image cleanup, artwork scan or storage policy work.

Current image rule

Supabase Storage is for images and artwork references.

Current video rule

Mux/HLS/public URLs are for videos. Supabase Storage is not the heavy video host.

Protection
