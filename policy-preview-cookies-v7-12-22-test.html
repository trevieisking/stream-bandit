Stream Bandit Cookie Policy — Draft

Contact: info@chatterfriendsstreambandit.co.uk

1. What this policy covers

This policy covers cookies and similar browser storage used by Stream Bandit. It also covers local storage or similar technologies that store or access information on a user device.

2. Necessary storage

Stream Bandit may use necessary storage for sign-in, security, app operation, theme choices, accessibility, player comfort, saved settings and safe navigation. Necessary storage is used to provide the service requested by the user.

3. Optional cookies/storage

Analytics, marketing, tracking or non-essential cookies should be off unless the user actively chooses them. Users should be told what these technologies do and why they are used.

4. Consent

Users should be able to choose necessary only, accept all optional cookies, or manage cookie settings. Consent should involve a clear positive action and should be easy to change later.

5. Third-party services

Stream Bandit may use services such as Supabase, Mux, GitHub Pages or future analytics/payment providers. Cookies or storage from those providers need to be checked before public launch.

6. Cookie audit needed

Before launch, Stream Bandit should list each cookie/storage item, purpose, provider, duration, whether it is necessary, and whether it processes personal data.

7. Changing choices

Users should be able to reset or change cookie preferences through the cookie settings area once final cookie controls are built.

8. Contact

Cookie questions: info@chatterfriendsstreambandit.co.uk.
