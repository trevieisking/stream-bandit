STREAM BANDIT CHILDREN / FAMILY WATCH POLICY — DRAFT Contact: info@chatterfriendsstreambandit.co.uk 1. Purpose This policy explains the draft rules for children, family-labelled accounts and Family Watch Bundle access. 2. Family Watch Bundle Family Watch should be viewing-first. It should include public/family-safe content, saved lists, search where suitable, accessibility controls and player comfort. It should not include creator tools, account billing, user management, admin pages or builder tools by default. 3. Child-suitable content Content marked for children or family viewing should be reviewed for age suitability, safety, privacy and harmful themes. Age ratings and kids-suitable labels should be used carefully. 4. No public posting by default Children/family accounts should not submit videos, create public channels, publish content, manage other users or access public creator/admin features by default. 5. Data minimisation Stream Bandit should collect only what is needed for the service, especially for children/family use. Optional tracking should not be enabled by default for child/family users. 6. Parent/guardian requests Parent or guardian requests about child/family accounts should be sent to info@chatterfriendsstreambandit.co.uk until a proper backend support workflow exists. 7. Safety review Before Stream Bandit is marketed to or likely used by children at scale, child privacy, online safety, reporting, moderation and parental-control duties should be legally reviewed. 8. Contact Child/family account requests: info@chatterfriendsstreambandit.co.uk.
