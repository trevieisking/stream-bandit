STREAM BANDIT CREATOR AND CONTENT RULES — DRAFT Contact: chatterfriends2021@gmail.com 1. Creator responsibility Creators must only submit videos, artwork, metadata, descriptions and links they own or have permission to use. 2. Review process Submitted content may be reviewed before publication. Stream Bandit may approve, reject, remove, hide or request changes to content. 3. Prohibited content Creators must not submit illegal, harmful, abusive, exploitative, hateful, dangerous, adult-mislabelled, rights-infringing, malicious or misleading content. 4. Metadata and artwork Titles, descriptions, ratings, genres, artwork and thumbnails should be accurate and safe. Misleading metadata may lead to rejection or removal. 5. Copyright and takedown Copyright, ownership and takedown concerns should be reported to chatterfriends2021@gmail.com. A dedicated process should be created before larger creator onboarding. 6. Loss of creator access Serious or repeated rule-breaking may lead to removal of submit privileges, content removal, account limits or cancellation review. 7. Contact Creator/content reports: chatterfriends2021@gmail.com.
