STREAM BANDIT PRIVACY POLICY — DRAFT Contact: chatterfriends2021@gmail.com 1. Draft status This privacy policy is a working draft for review before public launch, paid subscriptions or wider user onboarding. 2. Data Stream Bandit may process Stream Bandit may process account details, profile details, avatar and banner URLs, channel information, saved lists, watch progress, likes, favourites, submissions, review status, plan or feature settings, support requests and technical/browser information needed to run the service. 3. Why data is used Data may be used to provide accounts, display profiles and channels, save preferences, run video features, manage submissions, apply permissions, provide support, protect the platform and improve the service. 4. Children and family use Where Stream Bandit is likely to be accessed by children or used in a Family Watch context, data should be minimised, privacy-protective defaults should be used, and creator/admin/billing tools should not be available to children by default. 5. Cookies and browser storage Necessary storage may support sign-in, security, theme, accessibility, player comfort, menu state and user preferences. Optional analytics or marketing storage should require active consent and be easy to change. 6. Sharing data Stream Bandit should not sell personal data. Data may be processed through service providers needed to run the platform, such as hosting, database, storage, video playback or support tools. Any sharing must be reviewed before launch. 7. Retention Data should be kept only as long as needed for the service, legal obligations, safety, support, billing records or dispute handling. Exact retention periods need legal review. 8. User rights and requests Users may contact chatterfriends2021@gmail.com to ask about account data, corrections, access requests, deletion requests or privacy questions. Requests must be reviewed before action is taken. 9. Security Stream Bandit should use reasonable technical and organisational safeguards. Private keys and service credentials must not be placed in public frontend code. 10. Contact Privacy and data requests: chatterfriends2021@gmail.com.
