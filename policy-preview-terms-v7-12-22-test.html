Stream Bandit Terms of Use / End User Agreement — Draft

Contact: chatterfriends2021@gmail.com

1. About Stream Bandit

Stream Bandit is a video, channel, playlist, creator and website-builder style platform. Features may include watching videos, saved lists, creator submissions, channels, playlists, collections, forms, builders, pricing plans, user permissions and admin safety tools.

2. Draft status

This document is a working draft for review. It must be checked by a qualified legal professional before public launch, paid subscriptions or wider user onboarding.

3. Accounts

Users must provide accurate account information, keep login details secure and not allow others to misuse their account. Stream Bandit may limit, suspend or cancel access where an account breaks rules, creates legal or safety risk, abuses the service or attempts to bypass permissions.

4. Plans, bundles and paid features

Pricing plans, feature add-ons and bundles shown in Stream Bandit are draft until real billing is connected. When billing is launched, users should be shown clear plan terms, renewal terms, cancellation rights, support contact details and any usage limits before purchase.

5. Viewer and Family Watch use

Viewer and Family Watch accounts should be limited to appropriate watching, saved lists, accessibility/player comfort and account settings. Family or child-labelled use should not receive creator, public posting, billing, admin, user management or builder tools by default.

6. Creator use

Creators must only submit content, artwork, metadata and links they own or have permission to use. Stream Bandit may review, reject, remove or request changes to creator content. Approval is not guaranteed.

7. Prohibited use

Users must not upload, submit, publish or promote illegal, harmful, abusive, exploitative, hateful, misleading, unsafe, rights-infringing or malicious content.

8. Account and subscription requests

Users may request cancellation, account closure, plan changes, data requests or policy questions by emailing chatterfriends2021@gmail.com. A frontend form may prepare an email but does not automatically cancel, delete, refund or change any account until Stream Bandit confirms the request.

9. Cookies and privacy

Stream Bandit may use necessary browser storage for account or session state, safety, theme, accessibility, player comfort and app operation. Optional analytics or marketing cookies should require active consent.
