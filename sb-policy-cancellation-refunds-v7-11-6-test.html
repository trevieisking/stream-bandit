STREAM BANDIT CANCELLATION, REFUND AND ACCOUNT REQUEST POLICY — DRAFT Contact: info@chatterfriendsstreambandit.co.uk 1. Draft status This policy is a working draft. It must be reviewed before Stream Bandit takes real subscription payments. 2. How to request cancellation Users may request subscription cancellation, account closure, plan changes, data requests, deletion requests or policy questions by emailing info@chatterfriendsstreambandit.co.uk. A web form may prepare an email, but it does not automatically cancel or delete anything. 3. Information to include Users should include their name, account email, plan or bundle, request type, and enough detail for Stream Bandit to identify the account and request. 4. Confirmation A cancellation or account change should only be treated as completed after Stream Bandit confirms it in writing. 5. Refunds Refund terms are not final. Before paid launch, Stream Bandit must define refunds, cooling-off rights, digital content/service rules, renewal terms, payment timing and exceptions. 6. Access after cancellation Access may continue until the end of a paid period unless legal rules or final subscription terms require otherwise. This needs legal review. 7. Account deletion and data Account deletion or data removal may be limited by legal, billing, safety, dispute or fraud-prevention duties. Privacy requests should be reviewed before action. 8. Contact Cancellations and account requests: info@chatterfriendsstreambandit.co.uk.
