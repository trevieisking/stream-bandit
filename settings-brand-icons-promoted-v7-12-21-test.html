Logo upload flow

1. Drop image

Drag a PNG, JPG, WebP or similar image into the upload area. Clicking the area also opens the file picker as a fallback.

2. Upload

Admin upload sends the selected image to stream-bandit-images/global/.

3. Save globally

After upload, save the uploaded public URL into sb_app_settings.settings.logoUrl.
