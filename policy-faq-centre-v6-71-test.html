Creator Rules

  • Creators must only submit content they own or have permission to share.
  • Submissions should not go public until reviewed.
  • Metadata, artwork and video source must be truthful and safe.
  • Admin can request changes before publishing.
  • Repeat rule-breaking can remove creator access later.

FAQ Draft

What is Stream Bandit?

A streaming/video platform and future website builder for videos, channels, playlists and branded video sites.

How do I watch?

Use Home, Library, Genres, Channels, Collections, Playlists, Details and Player pages.

How do creators submit?

Creators use Submit Video. Admin reviews through Review Queue before public publishing later.

What are plans?

Plans will decide feature access, such as creator tools, builder tools and admin/site-owner tools.

What is Mux/HLS?

Mux/HLS/public URLs are planned for video playback. Supabase Storage is mainly for images.

Is payment active?

No. Plans and pricing are draft only until policies, costs and backend safety are ready.
