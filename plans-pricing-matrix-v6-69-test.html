💳 Fair Pricing for Stream Bandit

Research-backed draft pricing for Stream Bandit as a video hosting, streaming, creator-channel and website-builder platform. This page is planning only: no checkout, no billing, no plan changes and no live app effect.

Recommended model

Flat plan + clear usage allowance + optional add-ons.

Best launch range

Free, £24, £74, £229, Enterprise from £499.

Big risk

Do not hide expensive Mux usage inside cheap plans.

Not connected. Draft only.

Pricing research loaded. Draft only. No payment, billing, user, permission or access changes.
Executive summary: Stream Bandit should sit between business video hosting and full OTT/site-builder platforms. It is more than raw hosting, but should not charge Muvi-style enterprise prices until apps, SSO, deep operations tooling and enterprise support exist.
Recommended launch prices: Free Viewer £0; Creator Starter £24/month or £240/year; Creator Plus £74/month or £740/year; Pro/Site Owner £229/month or £2,290/year; Enterprise/Custom from about £499/month plus usage or implementation.
Important: Mux/HLS should be Pro, add-on or bring-your-own-video-engine on lower tiers. Lower plans need usage guardrails to protect costs.

Warn users at 80%, 90% and 100% usage. Starter should not silently overbill.
