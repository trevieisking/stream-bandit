Pricing Notes

  • Do not choose final prices until Mux usage, storage, bandwidth and support needs are clearer.
  • Start with plan labels before payment collection.
  • Keep free viewing simple so the app remains easy to try.
  • Charge for creator/site-owner tools only after policy, billing and support pages are ready.
  • Custom domains and heavy upload tools should be higher-tier or owner-only later.
